AMY'S PRIORITIES

Raise the Bar for Academic Excellence

Texas students deserve rigorous academic standards that prepare them to compete globally and lead locally.

Empower Parents Through Transparency

I will champion full transparency in curriculum and school policies, ensuring parents have a clear voice and visibility into what their children are taught.

Protect and Expand School Choice

Every Texas family deserves the freedom to choose the best educational path for their child—whether public, charter, private, or homeschool.

Strengthen Public Schools, Not Bureaucracy

I'll advocate for strong, well-resourced public schools that prioritize student achievement over administrative bloat.

Restore Truth in Teaching

I will fight for a return to fact-based curriculum that teaches real history, foundational civics, and the enduring principles of our republic.

Uphold Conservative Values in Education

I believe schools should reinforce respect, responsibility, and the traditional values that strengthen families and communities.
