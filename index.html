AMY'S PRIORITIES

Raise Academic Standards

Restore rigor to core subjects so every Texas student graduates ready for college, career, or service.

Keep classrooms focused on literacy, math, science, and civics—not political agendas.

Support local communities and educators with clear standards, resources, and accountability.

Curriculum Transparency

Open curriculum review and easy-to-access materials for families and taxpayers.

School Safety

Strong safety standards and mental health supports so students and teachers can thrive.
