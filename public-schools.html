Strengthen Public Schools, Not Bureaucracy

I'll advocate for strong, well-resourced public schools that prioritize student achievement over administrative bloat.

Outcomes Over Bureaucracy

Amy Bundgus understands that the true measure of a strong public school system is student achievement—not the size of its bureaucracy. Her focus is on outcomes that matter: higher proficiency scores, stronger graduation rates, and students who are genuinely prepared for life beyond the classroom.

Proven Systems Approach

With her background in global learning strategy and local school board leadership, Amy knows how to build systems that elevate instruction, support educators, and drive measurable academic success. She's committed to ensuring that every Texas student has access to a rigorous, transparent, and empowering education—because when students thrive, communities do too.

Strategic Investment in Excellence

Amy's approach focuses on strategic investments that directly impact student learning: qualified teachers, effective curriculum, modern resources, and accountability measures that work. She believes in cutting through bureaucratic red tape to ensure resources reach the classroom where they matter most. Her vision is clear: strong public schools that serve students first, not administrative convenience.

Resource Allocation
