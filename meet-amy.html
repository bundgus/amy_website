Meet Amy

A Texas mom, educator, and advocate for academic excellence.

Raised on a family farm, Amy Bundgus learned early the value of hard work, faith, and service to community. She moved to Texas over 20 years ago and dedicated her career to improving student outcomes—supporting teachers, partnering with parents, and championing high standards.

Amy and her family have deep roots in North Texas. As a mother, she understands the importance of safe schools, rigorous coursework, and transparent curricula. She believes the State Board of Education must set clear expectations that prepare students for the future while respecting local control.

On the Board, Amy will be a principled, solutions-focused voice for students, parents, and teachers. She will work to raise reading and math proficiency, expand high-quality career and technical education pathways, and encourage civics education that unites students around our shared Texas and American ideals.
